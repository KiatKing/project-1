Interest

Watch Making & Modding

My passion for building and creating manifested in my hobby of watch making. Designing and assembling a watch from scratch allows me to explore and mould my creative side.

The process of watch making, to me, is a form of meditation. It is where I can de-stress, clear my mind, and only focus on the task at hand.

Rock Climbing

Often we think of work and play as antonyms, opposite ends of life’s spectrum. Play, however, can become the key to training your mind to problem-solve more effectively. It’s no coincidence that my problem-solving skills have sharpen and improved since rock climbing became a part of my routine over the past 2 years.

Rock Climbing problems (yes, they’re literally called problems) force me to think creatively about how I need to sequence my moves. I’ve learned to go back to the drawing board as soon as I fall off the wall, rather than trying the same failed method twice.
